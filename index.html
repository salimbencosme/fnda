Our history

In 2006, because of an obvious need, teacher and psychologist Evely Peniche sacrificially established a makeshift classroom in their open-air garage. Evely started teaching 12 children in the afternoon. Good news travels fast and by the summer she hired a teacher to help as the number of children reached 50. By the mid 2007, Evely and her three teenager daughters moved from the first floor of their home to the second floor. By Aug 2008, the upper balcony of their home also became a classroom. Then in January 2010, the local government told Evely that she could no longer have a school in their home. Thankfully, a two storey rental building near their home came available for the school.

Then the Samaritan Foundation help us in building the school and in the actuallity we have a new second floor.

Today the staff is comprised of 16 teachers. The children range from age 3 to 16 and classes are given up to grade 11 of secondary. The hole children school actually is 385 children.

Mission

Our main focus is bringing support for the most needed people in the Dominican Republic, being our centre of attention education based on christian values in order to help vulnerable people to have a better life not only while here on earth but also in eternal life.

Vision

Nest of Love Foundation is a major force in transforming and growing individuals and comunities showing a way out of the poverty cicle.

Core values

Spiritual Development: We strive to be a spiritual development support for every single person we reach.

Culture of Collaboration and Excellence: working with cooperation as a team to achieve our goals, we challenge each other to reach the highest level of excellence.

Transparency: To be open with our associates, who trust in our work and those who believe that the change we are fighting for is impacting our world.

Integrity: We will adhere to moral and ethical principles.

Accountability: Carry out all our work with the greatest responsibility and delivering high quality outcomes.
